Graphic Design

As the Vice Chair of Design for outreach in SUBOG, I colaborate with the other 6 committees to create graphics for their events

The Brainstorming Process

  1. Receive fact sheet describing the event from my chairs
  2. Brainstorm with the chairs about what they envision for the graphic
  3. Finalize design of the graphic

The Design Process

  1. Use Photoshop to composite any portions of the background and export
  2. Use Illustrator to design any vector graphics and text on the image and export
  3. Use Lightroom to make adjustments to finalize the look
  4. Finalize design of the graphic
